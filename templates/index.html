For Diti

A little space for our big memories

The Premise

I started making this when u were sick. We weren’t talking a lot as you were resting and after a really long time I went to sleep without wishing you good night. I had only sent a message asking you to take care of yourself, unread. Sure, you’d gone “MIA” before — as Aayushmaan and I call it, but I always used to wish you good night anyway. However this time around aunty messaged on your behalf informing me that you’re sick. And so I realised she could read my texts and think I’m overly needy and desperate. Despite the fact that I am, I don’t want her to have that impression of me. Anyhow I was just lying on my bed thinking about the perfect gift for your birthday, which I haven’t been able to decide what could be. And so I eventually started thinking about all the memories I have with you. I can be so melodramatic sometimes. But anyhow, it hit me — what if I gave you these memories back? What if you had a way to go to these memories whenever you wanted to? So I got out of my bed. Told my mum I just had a terrific idea and needed to start writing stuff down. And just as I started typing this… guess what popped up?

There goes my premise...

The Daddy Incident

Ahh yes. What better way to start this than to bring up the iconic “daddy” moment. Truth be told. I don’t have the complete details lodged in my mind anymore. You have always been big on nicknames. I personally wasn’t a natural nickname user myself. Hadn’t seen people use them so often and so casually. And to see you call people honey and sweetheart so often made me quite amazed. Soon as we were talking you called me sweetheart, a natural occurrence at this point of our relationship but back then stuff like that used to catch me off guard. And so I made a joke saying - “I swear one of these days my mother is gonna see these texts and get the very wrong idea”. Made you laugh so hard with that one. God do I love it when you laugh. Makes me feel a weird sense of accomplishment when I bring happiness to your face. But yes you doubled down eventually saying "Maybe I’ll use double the nicknames to actually get you in trouble." Never thought that would actually become true. In fact, we’re way past double. But yes to your doubling down I replied - “Whatever floats your boat…. Sweetheart!” One of the most iconic things I’ve ever said to you. Absolutely love it. Made you so flustered with that one. But yes that definitely sparked the chain of nicknames between us.

Eventually I think you got comfortable with nicknames a bit too much. That's the thing that has been there between us since forever. You’ve always been ahead of me. At everything. We follow the same path and eventually I do catch up to you. But unfortunately or fortunately you always beat me with the check points. You called me daddy. Yep. I get that not knowing the “messed up” meaning might make it seem harmless, however as a person who knows well and good what that word stands for, it creeped the hell out of me. Idk about other people but as a virtuous person (relative term), I couldn’t stand having those kinds of conversations with a girl. Not the person I am and nor wanted to be. And I hadn’t really understood that you’re just truly innocent and didn’t mean to use the word with any bad intention. And due to that lack of understanding there I did a real pro gamer move. I blocked you. (Yeah... sighs while writing.) Made you so worried and panicked. I feel so terrible remembering that. God what you must have gone through the following night. All worried thinking if our slightly developing friendship went down the drain just like that. I mean cut to today where I’m literally ready to kill someone if it means I’ll get to talk to you and I don’t even have a topic to talk about. But anyhow as it turns out, our story was meant to go a lot ahead. Truly way ahead than I had imagined. So yes we met in school and I talked to Aayushmaan and then eventually to you. And after a million apologies I forgave you (don’t think I was mad really just being silly). I truly don’t get why I gave you such a hard time starting mein. You know there were so many times I had thought I should maintain my distance with this girl. But well. You are too irresistible. And honestly? I couldn’t be happier for that.

You Coming Over

Till this date I have no idea how it happened but it did. And credit where it's due — special mention to Vaibhavi, and a lil bit to Arnima too, I suppose. It truly was undoubtedly the most unexpected thing u had ever done for me up to that point. You know recalling that moment also made me realise that I myself haven’t really invited you here. Not really. Because if we truly think about it, Arnima was the one who initiated the idea as a joke and Vaibhavi was the one who persuaded you behind the scenes. So I’d like to change that and invite you over here. That too pretty soon I may add.

It was all honestly done in quite some fashion as well. How you reached here and Krish & me made you wait at the gate alone because our hair and skin was too important at the moment (horrible we are), and how I could barely spend any time with you despite me being the one who invited you here officially. Trust me, despite how much you think I was fixated over Arnima that day, I truly was in awe of the fact that Diti actually is here. Couldn’t be with you a lot and when we were free it got too late and we could be together a lot. But yeah, you being there meant a lot to me.

Maybe I didn’t truly appreciate the moment back nearly enough back then. But looking back I’m just glad for it to have happened. I don’t know exactly how good friends we were at that point but you coming to a place for a friend to watch a simple performance, definitely up there on the list of things you’ve done for me I wouldn’t think a friend would ever be close enough to me to do. Truly grateful I again am.

Happy for you, Not for me

So we fought. And we fought pretty bad actually. It happened because we were both being stupid and silly. In comparison I was probably worse but I’ve profoundly apologized for it multiple times and you’ve said you won’t talk to me anymore if I said sorry for that again. So for that I won’t say sorry here (I really am though).

It all started over something silly and I jokingly said no matter how mad you get, in the end you’ll just get desperate and come back to me saying SHAURYAAAAAAA. I mean looking back…. I see no lie here XD. But that probably isn’t the best thing a person who’s mad would like to hear. And so you started ignoring me. Leaving me on read and not replying. Now that's fair honestly you’re allowed to be mad but well you see; I’m immature. And so when you weren’t replying I again did my pro gamer move. Quite the person I am. But well that was only for like a very brief moment and I was annoyed at you not replying and wanted to annoy you back a little. But well when I did unblock you, you were even more mad and angry. Even I would be. And well it was a long conversation from there. And then a real silly thought hit me - is this friendship even worth continuing? I have no idea what came over me honestly. But you see I think what was going on in my mind was this is probably the closest friend I’ve ever had. You were the only person I had ever gotten so close to. Ever. Be it talking normally, sharing thoughts, feelings whatever. I’ve seen you be there for me whenever I’ve needed you and I wouldn’t say I had done the same a lot back but I was definitely willing to and was there whenever the opportunity presented itself (not having lunch together in school doesn’t count you know that's separate). But anyhow I said - “I think we should talk a little less” Now you know it, I know it. I’m basically ending things. I thought this was too much for me mentally. I can’t be connected to a person so much on an emotional level and then have so big fights with them for practically no reason at all. Didn’t seem good for my mental. And I can’t say this enough — I love you, I love you, and I love you sooo much for whatever you did ahead and not actually giving me what I had asked for. I can’t express this enough. I’m actually tearing up writing this. That night was sure one hell of a night. But not as bad as the next one.

When I said what I said, you simply asked a simple question - is that my solution to the problem? Idk why but it did make me reconsider everything. And fortunately I did take everything back. I was still mad at you for the rest of the night but by the morning I was fine. However you weren’t completely sure if I’m back to normal or not. I’ve told you this now and I'm pretty sure I got this from my father but I cool down as quickly as I heat up. Not entirely sure if it's good or bad but that's there. But you wanted to keep talking to me — about anything. Anything you could to make sure that I actually don't start “talking less”. Eventually we started talking about my performance. I started talking about how we could do things the next time. Maybe you and I could even perform together and you would come here again. However you said you might not be able to do that but you’ll be here to support me over a video call for sure. Me being confused asked why’s that? And that’s when you revealed your big plans of going abroad. Honestly it was quite the shocker. Like that genuinely hit me like a bus. I had never imagined and thought of you going away like that. I mean it's not like we would have stayed next door forever. We'd still go to college and very likely in different states at some point. But going abroad makes you practically inaccessible to me. Sure u can the argument of talking over video calls etc and meeting over the holidays, it simply isn’t the same. And that’s probably the first time it actually occurred to me how much you mean to me and how much I want you. You had cried a lot the night before and it was my turn now. I genuinely weeped like a baby after a pretty long time. And after two hours of crying, I typed the following message.

Not completely accepting things but letting you know my feelings in my own way which I’m happy you understood. And so when you woke up on the third day I had both of us in tiers. But that was probably the first time deep down I ever felt that I loved you. Didn’t accept it just yet. But deep down, I knew.

Farewell

13th of January 2025. Boy what a day that was. It's definitely on the top 3 days I’ve experienced in my life so far. It began with me jokingly telling you the day before how I won’t be spending time with you and will probably stick with Krish and the gang. Now I know this is gonna be hard to accept but I say this with truth that I always planned and wanted to spend the day with you. In 11th I wasn’t even close with you. Yet on farewell I for some reason I felt like spending some time with you. You seemed like a nice person. And Krish as well obviously but he spent the time with you for the most part of the day and I wasn’t close to you enough to come in between as you guys edited the farewell video. But yeah — one regret I had from last year was not spending the farewell with more friends. But I planned to change that and hopefully spend some time with at least two people who’ve really made my year worth remembering - you and Krish. Well we don’t need to talk about what happened with Krish. But I’m glad my real best friend was with me as much as you could be.

When I reached school, and you can verify this, you were the first person I called. You see, I knew the teachers weren’t gonna let us go in the same bus together. They’ll make us go classwise. And so I wanted to spend at least some time with you before we left. Unfortunately you were late that day. I just roamed around for 15 mins. Didn’t even wanna call Krish. And when I did, dumbass didn’t even pick up and I found him with Raunak. And then of course even in the bus he was with Shreya and Aditi and Raunak and I couldn’t even sit with my rest of the friends. You know the story, we don't need to make this too sad. You know at some point when I saw you down with Radhika and Gaurika, a part of me wanted to be like Ali and be a rebel and go sit with you in your bus. Like I probably wouldn’t even care what people would say. But then I saw sir send Ali back to his bus — and yeah, that was pretty disappointing. When we reached there we couldn’t be together because they made girls sit on the other side. And well there was the award ceremony, your speech and then eventually we did come back school.

When we reached back we were given around an hour to get our photos clicked. And my Princess is getting pictures clicked with all of her friends. And then she soon comes to me and asks for our picture. And I go: JuST yOu aND mE? You looked like you’d punch me. But thankfully because of people surrounding us I supposed you didn’t. And despite what I said there is nothing more I wanted that day than a picture of you and me. You actually looked so pretty that day. And yes, Vrinda clicked our picture — and boy, she ain’t the GOAT for no reason.

Probably my most favourite picture of us together. Absolutely love everything about it. Our on camera chemistry is pretty good I'm not gonna lie. But yes after that I didn’t have lunch with you. Not because I didn’t wanna have it with you but because I thought I should spend some time with my other friends as well. That’s the reason I tell myself to help myself sleep better while in reality I was just shy of your friends and didn’t wanna be tagging along with you too much cause I thought I might end up over doing it.

And now. The highlight of the evening. The princess danced on stage!!!

You told me you were gonna do it, but I didn’t expect you to do it that beautifully. Honestly? That probably made me fall in love with you right there — ngl. Truly justified your Ryan Princess award in classic Princess fashion. Truly beautiful.

We went to Mcdonalds after the farewell ended. Everyone was emotional and sad but I pulled you out in between while you were talking to someone because I had already called the taxi. And yeah then we went there and had a good time with Vrinda and Samarth. Loved that guy. And then eventually it was time to go home. That’s when something weird started to happen. I couldn’t explain if I tried but I was feeling something towards you. Like everyone I too wasn’t happy with the thought of school ending. But the thing is it's not the school that any of us were gonna miss. It was the people there. Without them, it was just a big building with red walls. Without the people it would just feel blue. But the thing is when we leave school we take one or two people from there with us, people who we plan to keep a part of our lives. That is probably what helps us go forward. You can’t bring everything from your past into your future. But having some meaningful fragments helps you move forward. You see, I didn’t have those fragments. I had a Jewel. And that jewel was you. And that jewel was soon about to go away. As my father was reaching closer to us, to pick us up and eventually drop us home, I could feel my heart sink. I wished this moment of me and you sitting together never ended. But the realisation that you’ll soon be going far far away from me, it almost killed a part of me. When we were about to get inside the car I told you: I’ll miss you. I seriously can not stress enough how many emotions in that moment through those words were being conveyed. I couldn’t cry in front of my father and so I pulled it together for a while, before eventually spending an undisclosed amount of time in the bathroom crying silently. That day was quite the roller coaster ride for me.

Even though now we both plan to go abroad, there’s no 100% guarantee of how close our fates will let us be. But I believe in will more than I believe in fate. And that belief is what tells me that you and me princess, we got a long road ahead of us.

Snapshots of the story so far...

Final Words

Well finally I'd just like to wish you all the happiness in the world. I’m not sure if you’re reading this on your birthday or a day before. (I might’ve gotten too desperate) I cried multiple times while writing this and I'm afriad that I might have made this a bit heavier than I should have. But I hope I could convey to you how much you mean to me and how much I love you. WISHING YOU THE HAPPIEST BIRTHDAY PRINCESS❤️
